Welcome to the 25th National Session of EYP Ireland!

Weclome to the website of the 25th National Session of EYP Ireland. This session will brought together over two hundred young Europeans in Cork, Ireland, from the 18th to the 23rd of April 2022. Over the course of the event participants had the opportunity to discuss pressing global issues that particularly affect Europeans. They also had the chance to engage with local and international stakeholders in order to increase the impact of their discussions on a number of different occasions throughout the length of the event.

This event is centered around the theme “Only the Beginning..”. This theme encouraged participants to consider the experiences they have thus far lived and reflect on them in such a way to better themselves moving forward in life. It also allowed us to acknowledge the roots of our organisation while looking forward to its future. We also produced material that we aim to share with the processes around the Conference on the Future of Europe. This material takes the form of resolution or white paper style documents that are the fruits of detailed discussions and debates over the span of five days.

The event also aimed to provide training for a number of volunteers in a broad range of spheres to encourage active citizenship, leadership, and many other soft skills. These training events happened both informally and non-formally in the months leading up to the event as well as the event itself. The trainers delivering these trainings have a wide spectrum of skills and experience which we believe provided an invaluable experience for both volunteers that help with the running of our event as well as the participants.

Our Session Video

The media team of the session composed a video that is supposed to capture the essence of the event. They have put a lot of effort in taking pictures and videos that may serve as a visual documentation of the event. Session videos mark a traditition at EYP session and are always shown at the closing ceremony at the event to celebrate the past few days and take time to reflect on the experiences made.
